Choose your own adventure! Pick the edition of Pattern Lab 2 that you'd like based on template language or environment:

  • Mustache Edition

    This edition of Pattern Lab uses Mustache as the templating engine.

  • Twig Edition

    This edition of Pattern Lab uses Twig as the templating engine.

  • Drupal Edition

    This edition uses Twig and includes some Drupal-specific additions for easy Drupal theme integration.

  • Thin Edition

    The thin edition serves as a clean base upon which you can build your own custom edition.
